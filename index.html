Blue Cross NC Product Portfolio

Enterprise-level analytics and platform solutions designed to advance population health, affordability, and self-service data accessibility.

MemberPulse Suite

Business Problem: Inability to categorize members into actionable segments for tailored care programs and outreach, leading to inefficiencies in care coordination and limited population insights.

Solution: Advanced analytics suite enabling population health segmentation and cohort management for 3M members across Blue Cross NC.

  • Scope: Power BI dashboards for program performance, member health insights, and ROI tracking; Streamlit-based interface for creating dynamic cohorts with advanced filtering; AI-driven clustering for member personas, Snowflake data layers.
  • Impact: Reduced analytics intake backlog by 70%, empowering 250+ users with real-time self-service capabilities, enabled targeted care campaigns, and reduced cost.
  • Technical Stack: M360 data layer with Snowflake backend, UI/UX point-and-click interface for non-technical users, export & sharing functionality
  • Innovation: Introduced proof-of-technology for Streamlit integration, enabling scalable data exploration, supported predictive modeling for rising risk and program ROI.

HC Executive Benchmarks (6-Blocker)

Business Problem: Executives lacked a unified lens across cost, utilization, and performance metrics, resulting in fragmented insights, delayed decisions, and missed affordability opportunities.

Solution: Unified executive benchmarking dashboard providing leadership visibility into enterprise performance, utilization, and cost trends.

  • Scope: Six analytic blocks covering Top Condition Trends (unit cost and volume), Inpatient Trend Monitoring, Case/Disease Management Performance, UM Metrics, Trend vs CoC Portfolio, and Executive Headlines & Focus Signals.
  • Impact: Eliminated fragmented reporting and reduced decision latency through centralized Power BI views.
  • Collaboration: Partnered with Affordability, Clinical, and Strategy leaders to define roadmap and success measures for aligning governance, analytics, and interventions and adoption.
  • Outcome: Drove enterprise alignment on performance signals, reducing duplicative analytics efforts, and highlighted underperforming programs and redirected investments to high-impact initiatives.

Cost of Care Insights (COCI)

Business Problem: Fragmented cost and utilization data limited visibility into financial drivers, preventing proactive cost management and forecasting accuracy.

Solution: Strategic analytics platform enabling proactive affordability management and root-cause cost driver analysis.

  • Scope: Integrated Cost Driver Lens (service lines, providers, and populations), Predictive & Root Cause Analytics (cost spikes and predictions on future utilization trends), Enterprise Affordability Impact Tracking (enterprise-level affordability measures and tracks savings against targets for transparency and accountablity) using data from claims, membership, provider, Milliman, ETG Data Mart.
  • Impact: Identified multimillion-dollar savings through early visibility into cost-of-care trends, supporting enterprise goals and value-based contracts.
  • Alignment: Embedded into enterprise affordability governance to guide proactive strategy.
